Job hunting is hard enough.

OnboardingSA pulls real vacancies into one clean, searchable place — and sends you straight to the employer to apply. No noise, no dead links, no games.

Why we exist

Openings are scattered across dozens of company websites, notice boards and social media posts. Good jobs get missed simply because people never see them in time. And with recruitment scams everywhere, it's hard to know what's real.

OnboardingSA fixes that. We gather genuine vacancies, present each one clearly, and link you directly to the employer's own application page — so you spend your energy applying, not hunting.

How it works

1

We list the job

A real, current vacancy — written clearly, with the location, requirements and closing date.

2

You find it fast

Search and filter by keyword, province, category and closing date to get to the right roles in seconds.

3

You apply at the source

One tap takes you to the employer's official careers page to submit your application directly.

Our promise to job seekers

OnboardingSA is, and will always be, free for job seekers.

  • We never charge you to view or apply for a job.
  • We never collect your CV or application on an employer's behalf — you apply directly with them.
  • Every listing links to the employer's official page, so you always know exactly where your application is going.

If any site ever asks you to pay to apply for a job, treat it as a scam and walk away.
