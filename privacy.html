Privacy Policy

Last updated:

OnboardingSA (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy and handles personal information in line with South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us — for example, your name, email address or phone number when you sign up for job alerts. We do not collect or store job applications or CVs; you apply directly on each employer’s official website.

How we use it

We use your contact details solely to send you the job alerts you asked for. You can unsubscribe at any time, and we will stop.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. If we display advertising in future, advertising partners such as Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers that help us operate (for example, an email or messaging provider), and only as needed to deliver the alerts you signed up for.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@onboardingsa.co.za.

Links to other sites

Our listings link to employers’ official websites. Once you leave OnboardingSA, that site’s own privacy policy applies.
